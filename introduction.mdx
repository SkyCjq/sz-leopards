## Getting Started

Thoughtly revolves around two important concepts: <b>Building</b> Agents and <b>Deploying</b> Agents.

### What is a Voice Agent?

A <b>Voice Agent</b> is a conversational interface that can interact with your customers over the phone, just like a human would. Agents can answer questions, provide information, and even take actions on your behalf.

As a brand new technology powered by cutting-edge <a target="_blank" href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Large_language_model">large language models</a>, building a Voice Agent is typically reserved for only the most experienced machine learning and telephony engineers. Thoughtly simplifies this process by providing a simple platform that anyone can enjoy.

## Use Cases

Thoughtly enables you to build advanced Voice Agent that can handle a wide variety of use cases. Here are some examples of what you can build with Thoughtly:

### Inbound Calls

When customers call your <a href="deploy/buying-a-number">Thoughtly phone number</a>, your Voice Agent will answer the phone and interact with them just like a human would.

Alternatively, you can <a>forward calls from your existing phone number</a> to your Thoughtly Agent, or use SIP to connect your Voice Agent to your existing phone system.
