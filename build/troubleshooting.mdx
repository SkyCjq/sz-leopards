## Troubleshooting

When building Voice Agents, you may encounter issues that prevent your Agent from working as expected. This guide will help you identify common issues and provide solutions to resolve them.

### My Voice Agent is randomly hanging up

If your Voice Agent is hanging up unexpectedly, it may be due to a misconfigured End Node. The End Node is where the conversation ends, and the Agent hangs up the call. If the End Node is not configured correctly, the Agent may hang up prematurely.
