**Become a Thoughtly Partner**  
We're excited to welcome you to the Thoughtly Partner Program! To join, you'll need to meet the following criteria:

- **Client Onboarding:** Successfully onboard a minimum number of clients to Thoughtly's platform.
- **Retention Rates:** Maintain strong client satisfaction and retention over a set period.
- **Usage Rates:** Ensure active and consistent usage of Thoughtly's services by your clients.

Once these criteria are met, we'll reach out to invite you to join.

**How to Get Started**  
1. Start your agency today.
2. Focus on meeting the program criteria above.
3. When you qualify, we'll connect with you to discuss the next steps.

In the meantime, explore our whitepaper, [“So, You Want to Start an AI Agency?”](/resources/whitepapers/agency-guide) to learn more about building a successful AI agency.
